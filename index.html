Bastille

**New Tour Dates Announced**

Bastille are an English pop rock band, who were formed in 2010.

After being signed to Virgin Records, they releasd their first studio album in March 2013 entitled 'Bad Blood' which reached the number one spot on the UK Album Chart and produced the hugley popular single 'Pompei', which reached number two in the UK Singles Chart.

In Novemebr 2019 they recorded a version of 'Can't Fight This Feeling' for the John Lewis and Waitrose Christmas advertisements. As of February 2021 they have released three studio albums and embarked on three sell out UK tours to accompany each album. They have sold over nine million records worldwide.
